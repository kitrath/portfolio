About

Chris Rathmel is currently enrolled in the KU Coding Boot Camp, a 24-week part-time web-development boot camp at the University of Kansas. He has a Master of Library Science degree from Emporia State University and has previously worked as a public librarian. For six years, he worked as an Information Specialist for a small research group in the Life Span Institute at the University of Kansas. While there, his responsibilities included technology training and support along the development and maintenance of multiple mental and behavioral health websites for Kansas residents. He has experience working with both WordPress and Drupal, and he developed an application with Python and React that was used across human service organizations in Shawnee County and Johnson County. Chris currently resides with his family in Lawrence, Kansas.
